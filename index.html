Overview

Purpose

My website aims to connect compassionate individuals with animals needing a loving home. We facilitate the adoption process by providing a user-friendly platform, ensuring that each animal finds a suitable and caring family.

Audience

The intended audience includes animal lovers of all ages looking to adopt a pet and those interested in learning more about animal care and welfare. Our site is mainly geared towards families, individuals, and seniors ready to open their homes and hearts to a new furry friend.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic Search and Filters: Use JavaScript to allow users to filter pets based on criteria such as species, age, size, and gender. You can update the list of available pets dynamically without needing to reload the page.
